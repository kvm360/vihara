About This Route

Taxi from Kottayam to Sabarimala

Kottayam — The Gateway to Sabarimala for Out-of-State Pilgrims

Kottayam Railway Junction is the primary arrival point for lakhs of Sabarimala pilgrims from Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, and other Indian states each year. Directly connected by rail to Chennai, Coimbatore, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Mumbai, Kottayam serves as the natural transit hub for the Sabarimala journey.

Sabarimala Season Calendar — When to Book

  • Mandalam Season: November to December (busiest period — advance booking essential)
  • Makaravilakku: January (extremely high demand — pre-book several days ahead)
  • Monthly openings: First 5 days of each Malayalam month
  • Vishu and Onam special openings

How Tamil Nadu Pilgrims Travel via Kottayam to Sabarimala

The most common route for Tamil Nadu devotees is train to Kottayam Junction followed by cab to Pampa. Trains from Chennai, Coimbatore, Salem, Madurai, and Tirunelveli connect to Kottayam. Pilgrims pre-book My Vihara Cabs on WhatsApp before boarding their train so the cab waits at Kottayam station on arrival.

Tips for Mandalam Season Pilgrims

  • Pre-book at least 2 to 3 days in advance during Mandalam peak months
  • Share your train number and expected arrival time at Kottayam station
  • Mention number of pilgrims and whether Erumely stop is required
  • Ask about return pickup at booking time for best rate
  • WhatsApp +91 94960 44589 in English, Tamil, Kannada, Telugu, or Hindi
