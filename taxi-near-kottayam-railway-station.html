Kottayam Railway Station (KTYM) — A Hub for All of Kerala

Kottayam Railway Station, station code KTYM, handles over 12,000 passengers daily. Located in Nagampadam, Kottayam, it is a key junction on the Ernakulam–Thiruvananthapuram line and connects Kottayam to Chennai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mumbai, and all major Indian cities. Out-of-state travellers — particularly Sabarimala pilgrims from Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Telangana — make KTYM one of Kerala's busiest intercity transit hubs.

Why Passengers Need a Reliable Taxi at KTYM

The auto-rickshaws outside Kottayam station are fine for short city hops but unsuitable for airport runs, pilgrimage routes, or outstation trips. Pre-booking a cab via WhatsApp before your train departs ensures your transport is secured without haggling at the station exit — especially important during Mandalam and Makaravilakku seasons when demand is extreme.

Trains Connecting to Kottayam Station — Key Routes

  • Chennai Egmore / Chennai Central — multiple trains daily, 12–14 hrs
  • Bengaluru City — overnight trains, 12–13 hrs (major source of Sabarimala pilgrims)
  • Hyderabad / Secunderabad — 22–26 hrs (large South Indian pilgrim group)
  • Mumbai CST / LTT — 40–44 hrs (Maharashtra pilgrims)
  • Ernakulam / Cochin — 1.5 hrs (local connection)
  • Thiruvananthapuram — 3 hrs (daily commuter and intercity route)
